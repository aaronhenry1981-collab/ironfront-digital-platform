# Iron Front Digital Platform - Version History

## v1.0.0 - Launch Lock (Current)

**Release Date:** December 2024

**Status:** LOCKED - Public UX frozen

### What's Locked

- Public-facing pages (Home, Pricing, Scale, Launch, Ecosystems, Apply)
- Pricing structure and tiers
- Apply flow and routing
- Stripe product configuration
- Legal pages (Privacy, Terms, Security)

### What Can Change

- Internal operator workflows
- Backend improvements
- Bug fixes
- Performance optimizations
- Features based on **real user feedback** or **revenue justification**

### Launch Criteria

Launch is defined as:
- 5-10 real users
- Paying or applying
- Giving feedback
- Completing flows without hand-holding

### Operating Priorities (Post-Lock)

1. **Traffic**
   - Direct outreach
   - Warm introductions
   - Controlled soft traffic

2. **Conversations**
   - What do people ask?
   - Where do they hesitate?
   - What do they misunderstand?

3. **Signal Collection**
   - Drop-offs
   - Tier selection patterns
   - Ecosystem interest

---

**Note:** This version represents the first stable public release. All future public UX changes require user feedback or revenue justification.
